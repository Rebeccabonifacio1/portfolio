Graphic Design



My interest in Graphic Design originally stemmed from my election to Media & Communications Coordinator for a student organization in 2019. The position required me to create, purchase & distribute authentic and original merchandise designs for current and past members. During this time, Student Ambassadors sold the most items in history of the organization and 87% of those who were offered items, purchased. From this success, I was asked to design James Madison University’s class of 2019 senior shirt, which sold nearly 800 items at JMU’s bookstore. After holding this position, I looked for other ways to implement Graphic Design into my life. With the help of Adobe Illustrator, I began using design as a way to recognize important people in my life, by taking special photographed moments and illustrating them.
