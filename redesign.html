Cleod9 Music


Over this past summer, I interned with Cleod9 Music, a group that provides music scores for movies and documentaries. My work varied in nature, but one of my two big focuses was redesigning Cleod9 music’s website. I worked closely with the CEO, Ian McLeod, to develop a working site that would meet the users needs and fall inline with Cleod9’s aesthetic and personal brand. I focused on making the site accessible, rebranding the previous work and contact pages, in addition to redesigning the color scheme and home page.The site, created with squarespace, was my first real experience within the site builder and I was impressed with ease of use and ability to add custom CSS.

Student Ambassadors


In January of 2019, I was elected to be the Media & Communications Coordinator for Student Ambassador’s, JMU’s volunteer tour guide organization. This position consisted of creating merchandise, taking photos, posting on social medias, and rebranding our online presence, most specifically redesigning our website. As a sophomore in college, this was really the first time I had tackled web design in any capacity, and was actually one of the main experiences that drove me to UX. Our previous site was rarely updated and difficult to navigate, so I focused on design layout and easy of use. Our site is mainly used to recruit new members, so I redesigned the site with that audience in mind, working to show them that Student Ambassadors is a professional, but fun organization! The site has since gone through other historians and therefore seen design changes, but my original stylistic, layout, and overarching content redesigns are still in tact.
