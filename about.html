About Me



Who I am:

I am a student first and foremost, but also a designer, an advocate and a creative. I am lucky enough to have multiple different interests that I have passion and drive for and I intend to bring a unique twist to any project I take on. I pride myself on being a constant learner, who attempts to find the good in everyday.



What I do:

I work with and among different media arts to help promote, and recognize people, places, and things. I have extensive previous experience with social media and have learned to use Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn as a tool for marketing. From this work, I have discovered the art of UX Design and Creative Advertising as a means for promotion. Most importantly, I work to maintain a positive and professional atmosphere for a variety of different organizations, student involvements and personal pages alike. I am always looking for new opportunities within these creative faculties and am ready to take on any challenge.



What I believe in:

Within and outside of my professional work, I value honesty, confidence and taking the road less traveled. I have a strong voice when advocating for the things I care about and believe that listening and understanding is the key to success. In my free time you can find me hiking through National Parks, learning about different conspiracy theories or creating new playlists on Spotify. At my core, I believe in being the change I wish to see in the world and intend to instill this value into any task I take on
