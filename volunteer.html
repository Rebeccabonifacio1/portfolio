In the interest of protecting public health, Arati for Congress is cancelling all in-person events, including canvasses.


Yet even as we take necessary precautions to prevent the spread of coronavirus, our organizing efforts will continue.


Here's how you can help from home, in three easy ways:

  • Sign up for phone banking shifts so that voters in NJ-5 keep hearing about our campaign & joining our movement.
  • Hop on a Social Team webinar to learn how you can help us spread the word online to as many voters as possible.
  • Join our online community of volunteers to stay in the loop about new opportunities

And are you a high school or college student interested in helping out while you're stuck inside? Join our Fellowship Program!

We've built incredible momentum due to the hard work you've put in so far. Let's continue onward and elect a progressive scientist in NJ-5!
